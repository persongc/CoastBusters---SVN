User Stories:
1. As a busy student, I want a way to search for trails based on shortest 
   length of time to complete, so I can get some quick exercise in between 
   classes.
2. As a user, I want a way to get the directions to the trail, so that I know 
   how to get there.
3. As a visitor to California, I want a way to search for trails in the area 
   that I will be staying, so that I can plan my trip in advance.
